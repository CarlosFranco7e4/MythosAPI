Commands:

GET

/mythos/{id}

-> Get an specific Mythos story

/mythos/name/{mythosId}

-> Get a list of stories in an specific Mythos

/mythos

-> Get all the Mythos stories

/mythos/comptar/{mythosId}

-> Get the quantity of stories in an specific Mythos

POST

/mythos

-> Post a story

DELETE

/mythos/{mythosId}

-> Delete a story

Put

/mythos

-> Modify a story
